Cloud connectivity and protocols for the Internet of Things

The Nordic Semiconductor Tech Webinar on Cloud connectivity and protocols for IoT provides an introduction to the protocols and an overview over the important factors you need to consider when developing a cellular IoT product. It aims to help you in choosing the right solution for sending your product’s data to the cloud.

The webinar is also available as an interactive, browser-based presentation and as a PDF including the notes.
